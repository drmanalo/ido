Ceremony

As the clock strikes noon, the doors open wide,

Bourton Hall welcome awaits, with hearts filled with pride.

For those staying the night, take your time, settle in,

Let the day’s magic gently begin.

 

The vows we exchange, the love we proclaim,

In an intimate space, by candle’s soft flame.

With limited sixty seating capacity, our hearts will unite,

A love story unfolds, timeless and divine.

 

But love knows no bounds, and neither does cheer,

Wedding reception begins as twilight draws near.

The bar opens at six and music will play,

Join us for sunshine, drinks, games, ice cream and Pimms.

 

As music sways and love takes its flight,

We will all dance beneath the soft moonlight.

A cherished Filipino tradition of moment so sweet,

Where laughter and love twirl at our feet.

 

If you wish to share a blessing so bright,

Clip a note or more notes as we dance through the night.

A token of joy, with wishes come true,

For the start of matrimonial life was made for us two.

 

So bring an extra bill, can be big or small,

And join in the fun dance, there is room for us all.
