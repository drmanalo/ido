Honeymoon Fund

Your love and presence are the greatest gifts,

But if you wish to send us adrift,

A honeymoon dream, a journey so bright,

A gift of adventure, a love-taking flight.

Not pots, not pans, nor things to store,

But memories we'll cherish for forevermore.

Should you wish to gift us this way?

We'll appreciate contribution to send us away.
